Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the most successful football players of all time, and this website provides some basic information about him

His career

  • Ronaldo began his senior career with Sporting CP, before signing with Manchester United in 2003
  • Ronaldo was the subject of the then-most expensive association football transfer when he signed for Real Madrid in 2009 in a transfer worth €94 million (£80> million)
  • One of the most known things regarding him is his rivalry with Lionel Messi

His achievements

  • He has scored a record 885 senior career goals for club and country. Moreover, he is one of the few recorded players to have made over 1,200 professional career appearances.
  • He has won 33 trophies in his career, including seven league titles, five UEFA Champions Leagues, the UEFA European Championship and the UEFA Nations League.
  • He has also won 1 Puskas award in his life
